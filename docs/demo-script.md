# ClassBridge — Demo Script (2:30)

## 0:00–0:20 — The Problem

"Every day, millions of students open their worksheets and hit a wall — not because they're not smart enough, but because the language is too complex, the text is too dense, or it's written in English when they think in Tamil or Hindi. ClassBridge fixes this. Let me show you."

## 0:20–0:50 — Upload & Demo Selection

"I'm going to use a prepared demo worksheet — a Grade 8 science worksheet on photosynthesis. Watch what happens when I click 'Generate Learning Pack.'

[Click demo worksheet → Generate]

The AI is now reading the worksheet, understanding the content, and building a personalized learning pack — all in seconds."

## 0:50–1:20 — Simplified Explanation & Translation

"Here's the simplified explanation — written at Grade 7 level in plain language. No jargon, no confusing sentences. And if I switch to Tamil — here's the same explanation side-by-side. Every sentence is translated so the student can compare."

[Switch to Translation tab, show side-by-side]

## 1:20–1:50 — Study Cards & Read-Aloud

"Now let's look at the study cards. These are flip cards — front shows the question, back shows the answer. Each one covers a key concept.

And see this 'Read Aloud' button? The student can listen to any explanation using their browser's built-in text-to-speech. No extra software needed."

[Flip a card → Click Read Aloud]

## 1:50–2:20 — Practice & Accessibility

"Practice mode has four question types — multiple choice, short answer, and explain-in-your-own-words. Every wrong answer gets supportive feedback — never shame.

And here — the accessibility panel. Font size, dyslexia-friendly spacing, high contrast, reduced motion. Every student can customize their experience."

[Open accessibility panel → adjust font size → toggle dyslexia mode]

## 2:20–2:40 — Impact & Closing

"ClassBridge isn't just a homework helper. It's an accessibility tool for the 2 billion students worldwide who learn in a language that isn't their first. It's for students with dyslexia, ADHD, or reading difficulties. It's for schools that don't have tutors or special education support.

Every worksheet deserves to be understandable. ClassBridge makes that real."
